About Miss Ani

Hi

I'm Ani — and Armenian is more than a language to me. It's the sound of my grandmother's stories, the thread that ties generations together, the quiet inheritance we carry in our hearts.

For as long as I can remember, I've dedicated myself to passing that thread to the next generation — one child at a time. With a foundation in Early Childhood Studies and Montessori education, I don't just teach Armenian. I create a space where children fall in love with it.

Every lesson is designed to feel like discovery. I draw on my background in media to bring language alive — through movement, storytelling, and the kind of joyful chaos that makes children forget they're learning. And because every child sees the world differently, my Montessori approach ensures that each one is met exactly where they are.

How I teach

Lessons that feel like play — because that's how children actually learn.

Armenian doesn't have to feel like homework. In my lessons, it sounds like a favorite song, a silly game, a story told just for your child. That's how language finds its way in — and stays.

Every session is built around your child: their age, their pace, their connection to their roots. Whether you're raising a fluent speaker or simply want your child to say barev to their grandparents with confidence — there's a place here for your family.

Meet online from anywhere in the world, or in person if you're in Philadelphia. No rigid curriculum. No one-size-fits-all. Just warm, joyful lessons that children actually look forward to.

Why Learn Armenian?

Discover the amazing benefits of learning one of the world's oldest and most beautiful languages

Boost Brain Power

Learning a second language improves memory, problem-solving, and creative thinking in children.

Ancient Heritage

Connect with over 1,600 years of rich history. The Armenian alphabet was created in 405 AD!

Family Connection

Help your child communicate with grandparents and relatives in their native language.

Songs & Stories

Discover beautiful Armenian folk songs, fairy tales, and traditions that bring the language alive.

Unique Alphabet

39 beautiful letters, each with artistic curves. Kids love learning "bird letters" — Trchnagir!

Global Community

Join millions of Armenian speakers worldwide and open doors to a vibrant diaspora community.
