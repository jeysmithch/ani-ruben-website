Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Ani Ruben (“we,” “us”) handles information when you use aniruben.org.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name (parent or guardian)
  • Your email address
  • Your child’s name (optional field on the form)
  • Your child’s age
  • Any message you choose to include

How we use your information

We use this information only to respond to your inquiry and to schedule or discuss a free trial lesson. We do not use it for unrelated marketing without your consent.

Third-party services

Our site relies on the following third parties to operate the contact form:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers form submissions to us. See Formspree’s privacy policy.
  • Google reCAPTCHA — helps protect the form from spam. See Google’s privacy policy.

Data sharing and sales

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email us at hello@aniruben.org.
